About Me

Hi, I'm Soumitri, a photography student based in Singapore. I first picked up a DSLR in 2017 and have a a passion for the visual arts. My current interests lie in photojournalism, fashion, fine-art and street photography. I am at a phase in my career where I would love to explore other areas of photography. I look forward to learning more about myself, my subjects and the world around me through photography.
